Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data.

Violet Keystone only collects data necessary to improve your experience. This may include information you provide directly (such as your name and email) and app usage data to help us enhance our services.

Data Protection

Your data is stored securely with advanced encryption methods. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Security is at the heart of what we do.

Data Sharing

We may share limited information with trusted partners to provide essential features, but only under strict confidentiality agreements. Your privacy always comes first.

User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us if you wish to manage your information or have questions about our practices.
